Partner pricing — here are three models to consider:
The smartest option is probably "Partner joins free for 3 months, then 50% off forever as long as the primary account is active." This way the primary user feels like they're giving their partner a gift, the partner gets hooked during the free period, and you're still earning from both accounts long term. It also makes users want to invite their partner, which is free marketing for you.
You could also do "Linked accounts: buy one, get partner 50% off" shown as a bundle at checkout — clean and simple.
The build order at the bottom is intentional — I told Replit to fix payments and the AI chatbot first, then roses and streaks, because those are what will retain users from day one. The lifestyle features (travel, outfits, journal) will layer on top of a working core.
